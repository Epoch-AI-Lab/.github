# Epoch AI Lab

Independent research for controlled model change.

Epoch AI Lab studies preservation-aware post-training: how to adapt small open models while measuring what changed, what stayed intact, and what should remain restricted.

## Release Boundary

We publish claims, code paths, and aggregate evidence where safe.

We do not publish exact ablation coordinates, bypass recipes, or details that make safety removal easier.
